### **Custom Photography Concept**

#### **Ideal Hero Shot**
- **Location**: Thika Superhighway or Nairobi Expressway
- **Vehicles**: Your actual fleet (3-4 vehicles)
- **Time**: Golden hour (sunset)
- **Angle**: Elevated view showing road and vehicles
- **Elements**: Kenyan landmarks in background

#### **Supporting Images Needed**
1. **Vehicle Categories**: Each type in Kenyan settings
2. **Customer Stories**: Real customers with vehicles
3. **Locations**: JKIA, hotels, business districts
4. **Team**: Your staff and facilities
5. **Process**: KYC, vehicle handover, etc.
